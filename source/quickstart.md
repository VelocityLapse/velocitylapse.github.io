# Quick Start

Once you've downloaded Velocity Lapse from Google Play, launch the app and you will see the home screen.

At the bottom of the screen are three tabs, enabling you to create time-lapses in three different ways.

- The **Timelapse** tab allows you to create time-lapses with a regular capture interval.
- The **Photolapse** tab allows you to capture photos manually to create time-lapses over long periods of time.
- The **Import** tab allows you to select images or a sequence of images to import and assemble into a time-lapse video.

For both the Timelapse and Photolapse tabs, tap the large blue button to create a new project. You will be launched into the respective interface which is specifically designed for capturing different types of time-lapses.
